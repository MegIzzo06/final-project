Volunteer Work

I've done lots of community service and volunteer work throughout my years in the National Honor Society (NHS) and Junior Reserve Officer Training Corps (JROTC) in highschool.
All of it ranges from Thanksgiving meal service to highway cleanup, though none of those relate to my degree.

The closest volunteer work relating to technology was when I volunteered as part of the tech team for JROTC Leadership and Academic Bowl.
We managed the cameras, microphones, sounds, livestreams, and some competition equipment.
